Roles of Volleyball Positions

Outside Hitter (OH)

The primary attacker and passer. Plays on the left side of the court, needing strong jumping and hitting abilities.

Opposite Hitter (OPP)

Plays on the right side. Responsible for hitting from the right and blocking the opponent's outside hitter.

Middle Blocker (MB)

The team's first line of defense. Tallest players, responsible for blocking and executing fast attacks from the middle.

Setter (S)

The "quarterback" of the team. Runs the team's offense, aiming to receive the second touch and set up the attackers.

Libero (L)

A specialized defensive player. Wears a different color jersey and can substitute freely in the back row to pass and dig.
